Something About me

I’m Dr. Hari Mohan Gaur, completed my PhD (Engineering) from NIT Kurukshetra and is currently working with School of CSET, Bennett University, India. He likes to work, grow and contribute best for budding researchers, engineers and associated organisation for the advancement of the society. He believes that we do not make our career; it lies in the way you work. His research areas includes Quantum circuits, Reversible computing, DFT, Fault tolerance and Resource Management in cloud environment. and Gatsby Computational Neuroscience Unit.

I completed my PhD at the Functional Imaging Lab of the German Primate Center, where I studied the effects of anesthesia on brain function using neuroimaging techniques, including functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging (fMRI) and in vivo 2‐photon calcium imaging.

I care about promoting open, collaborative, and reproducible science. In my free time, you will find me dancing to swing music and trying to play the saxophone.
